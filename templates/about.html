ABOUT D-PHISH

Our Mission

D-PHISH is committed to providing cutting-edge cybersecurity protection through advanced artificial intelligence and machine learning. Our mission is to create a safer digital environment by detecting and preventing phishing attacks before they cause harm. We believe everyone deserves protection from cyber threats, which is why we've built a powerful, easy-to-use platform accessible to all.

What We Do

We provide real-time phishing URL detection using a hybrid approach combining machine learning models with heuristic analysis. Our system analyzes 29 different URL features including domain characteristics, SSL certificates, page content, and behavioral patterns to deliver high-accuracy detection with minimal false positives. All processing is done locally without relying on external APIs, ensuring privacy and speed.

Our Technology

D-PHISH uses a state-of-the-art CatBoost machine learning model achieving 97.88% accuracy, enhanced with custom heuristic detection algorithms. Our system analyzes URL structure, domain reputation, SSL certificates, page content, and behavioral patterns. We continuously update our phishing blocklist and detection patterns to stay ahead of evolving threats. All analysis happens locally, ensuring your privacy while delivering lightning-fast results.
